Why BFR ?

BFR means Big Fucking Rocket is the next heavy launcher of SpaceX, the Elon Musk company in the aerospace sector. This new launcher aims to replace the Falcon 9, Falcon Heavy and the Dragon capsule used to refuel the International Space Station in the long term.

Accessibility

The Big Fucking Rocket is only accessible in the Biggest City, like New-York, Dubai, London or Shanghai.

Time and Distance

With the BFR distances our relationship to distances will completely change, indeed in the future we could live in New York and go to work in Shanghai.
